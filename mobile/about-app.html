Welcome to NourishMe!

NourishMe, created by GoldenLeaf Wellness Inc., is your personal companion for healthy, balanced, and easy-to-prepare meals. Designed especially for older adults, the app helps you plan meals that suit your lifestyle, health needs, and taste preferences — whether it’s low-sodium, heart-healthy, diabetic-friendly, or just wholesome and delicious.

Why You’ll Love It

  • Get daily or weekly meal suggestions that fit your health goals and lifestyle
  • Follow simple, step-by-step recipes in large, easy-to-read fonts
  • Receive gentle reminders for meal prep, hydration, and self-care
  • Track nutrients so you can see how every meal supports your health
  • Invite family or caregivers to help with planning and grocery lists if you like

Who We’re Here For

  • Seniors aged 60+ who want to eat well and stay independent
  • Those managing health conditions like diabetes, heart disease, or high blood pressure
  • Family members, caregivers, or healthcare professionals helping loved ones stay nourished

Our Friendly Design

  • Minimal, clean layout with large, easy-to-see buttons
  • Readable, rounded fonts like Nunito or Open Sans for stress-free reading
  • Warm and inviting colors: soft green for health, sunny yellow for positivity, and calm beige for clarity
  • Encouraging messages to keep you motivated: “You’re doing great! Keep nourishing yourself!”
  • Simple navigation so you can focus on what matters — enjoying your meals and feeling healthy

Our Mission

NourishMe is here to empower you to take charge of your nutrition with confidence. Our goal is simple: make healthy eating easy, stress-free, and enjoyable. Every feature, from meal planning to reminders, is designed to support your well-being and independence.

NourishMe isn’t just an app — it’s your partner in healthy living, helping you enjoy every meal with confidence and joy.
